## admission_eligibility
The Fellowship is geared towards IT students in their final year of education and graduates and professionals with a background in computer science and IT.
Eligibility requirements:
- Completion of courses in linear algebra, probability, statistics, and basic calculus
- Completion of programming and computer science courses
- Proficiency in Python programming
- Proficiency in English
- Ability to work well with others, strong communication skills, and a positive learning attitude

- Who can apply for undergraduate programs?
- Do I need to have finished Grade 12 to apply?
